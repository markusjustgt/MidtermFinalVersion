Top 3 Leonardo DiCaprio Movies

These are the Top 3 movies starring one of the greatest actors in our lifetime, Leonardo DiCaprio

Number 3: Titanic

In this spectacular dramatization from the real-life event, we saw the young DiCaprio burst into the scene, literally. This is a dramatic story about when Titanic sank in 1912.

Number 2: Wolf of Wallstreet

As in Titanic, we see DiCaprio starring in a movie based on a real-life event. This time we see him as Jordan Belfort, the infamous stockbroker.

Number 1: Once Upon a Time In Hollywood

Starring together with starts such as Margot Robbie and Brad Pitt, we see all the qualities DiCaprio has in his toolbox. Playing the fictional character Rick Dalton, we are witnessing one of the greatest actor performaces of the century.
